Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about buying, renting, and selling.

How do I search for properties?

To search for properties, use the search bar on the homepage. Enter your desired location, property type, and any other relevant criteria. You can also use the advanced search options for more specific filters.

What are the different property types available?

We list a wide range of properties including single-family homes, apartments, condos, townhouses, and land. You can filter by property type in your search to narrow down the results to what you're looking for.

How can I schedule a viewing?

Once you find a property you're interested in, you can request a viewing directly from the property details page. Click the "Schedule a Tour" button and select a date and time that works for you. The listing agent will then confirm the appointment.

What is the process for making an offer?

Making an offer typically involves working with a real estate agent who will prepare a formal offer document. This document includes the price you're willing to pay, as well as any contingencies. Contact one of our affiliated agents to get started.

How do I contact a real estate agent?

You can find agent contact information on each property listing. Additionally, our "Find Pros" section allows you to search for agents in your area based on their specialty and client reviews.
